📘 Meaning of Approximation
Maana ya Approximation

Approximation is a process of finding a number that is closest to the exact value.
Approximation ni njia ya kupata namba iliyo karibu zaidi na thamani halisi.

It involves expressing a number into a higher or lower value which is close to the exact value.
Inahusisha kuandika namba kwa thamani ya juu au ya chini ambayo iko karibu na thamani halisi.

Approximation can also be called Estimation and is represented by the symbol ≈.
Approximation pia huitwa Estimation na huonyeshwa kwa alama ≈.

We do approximation by rounding off numbers.
Tunafanya approximation kwa ku-round off namba.

Example: 47 ≈ 50
Mfano: 47 ≈ 50, kwa sababu 50 ndiyo karibu zaidi.
